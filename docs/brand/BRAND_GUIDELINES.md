## üìù Typography

## üéØ Logo Usage

### Logo Variants
1. **Full Logo:** "Circuit Prompt AI" with circuit icon
2. **Short Logo:** "Circuit Prompt" with icon
3. **Icon Only:** Circuit icon mark
4. **Wordmark:** Text only, no icon

### Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space of 2x the height of the logo on all sides.

### Minimum Size
- **Digital:** 120px width minimum
- **Print:** 40mm width minimum
- **Favicon:** 32x32px minimum

### Color Variations
1. **Primary:** Electric Violet gradient (#C084FC ‚Üí #06B6D4)
2. **Monochrome Dark:** Black or dark gray
3. **Monochrome Light:** White (on dark backgrounds)
4. **Outline:** Transparent with brand color stroke

### Don'ts
‚ùå Don't stretch or distort the logo
‚ùå Don't change the colors outside brand palette
‚ùå Don't add effects (drop shadows, glows, etc.)
‚ùå Don't rotate the logo
‚ùå Don't place on busy backgrounds without clear space

## üí¨ Voice & Tone

### Brand Personality
- **Innovative:** Cutting-edge, forward-thinking
- **Authentic:** Genuine, honest, transparent
- **Empowering:** Enabling creativity and freedom
- **Friendly:** Approachable, supportive
- **Technical:** Knowledgeable, precise

### Communication Style

**DO:**
- Use clear, concise language
- Emphasize innovation and real technology
- Highlight emotional authenticity
- Be transparent about capabilities
- Celebrate user creativity

**DON'T:**
- Use overly technical jargon
- Overpromise or exaggerate
- Be condescending or dismissive
- Hide limitations or constraints
- Use generic AI marketing speak

### Tone by Context

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Marketing | Inspiring, bold | "Create AIs that truly understand you" |
| Documentation | Clear, helpful | "Here's how to configure emotions..." |
| Error Messages | Friendly, solution-oriented | "Oops! Let's fix that together." |
| Success States | Celebrating, encouraging | "Amazing! Your AI is ready to chat." |
| Community | Inclusive, supportive | "We'd love to see what you create!" |
